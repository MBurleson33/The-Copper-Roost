Welcome

A Place to Gather & Make Memories

Specializing in a family-friendly atmosphere, The Copper Roost is a one-of-a-kind barndominium retreat boasting 5,000 sq. ft. of space with something for everyone to enjoy. It's the perfect place to get away with your family or friends.

With the ability to sleep up to 16, it's the ideal spot for family vacations, getaways, retreats, and events. This stunning 5-bedroom barndominium features 4.5 bathrooms with 4 of those bathrooms en-suite for maximum comfort and privacy.
