Things You Should Know

Checkout

Before You Leave the Roost

  • Strip All Beds & Towels Strip all beds and place towels in bedroom doorways before you leave.
  • Adjust Thermostats Set thermostats to 78°F during summer and 64°F during winter.
  • Run the Dishwasher Load and start the dishwasher before you head out.
  • Take Out the Trash Take all trash to the outside trash can.
  • Lights & Remotes Ensure all lights are off and return all remotes to where you found them.
  • Lock All Doors Lock all doors upon your departure.
  • Propane Tank Ensure the propane tank on the grill is turned all the way to OFF.
Check-Out Time

Check-out time is strictly 11:00 a.m. Should you require a later check-out we will do our best, but you need to let us know beforehand. If we have guests arriving that day, it will be almost impossible to offer a late check-out due to the cleaners needing to prepare for those guests.

We hope your stay was everything you hoped for.
